Overfitting in models

Say in a housing dataset there were two mansions that had doors with a green paint cover.

A risk models have is overfitting * So the model jumps to the conclusion that all houses with green doors are good houses

Not necessarily the case.

Risks of data

With increase in data comes risks of overfitting, or disparate impact from predictions
